How to Play

1. Drag block shapes from the bottom dock yard onto the 9x9 board.

2. Complete full rows or columns to clear blocks and score points.

3. Clearing multiple lines simultaneously triggers high-scoring combo bonuses!

4. Keep space open! The game ends when no more block layouts can be placed on the board.
